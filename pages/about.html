Our Story

Born from a love of time, craft, and the wrists that carry both.

Who We Are

Time Is a Canvas

Pacedall Watches was founded with a single belief: the watch face you stare at dozens of times a day should be something you're genuinely proud to wear.

We are a Pacedall Labs company — and like everything Pacedall builds, we obsess over the intersection of design, performance, and technology.

Our designers come from traditional watchmaking, UI design, and motion graphics — bringing a perspective that's rarely found in the digital watch face world.
