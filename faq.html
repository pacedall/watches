FAQ

Quick answers for installing, using, and troubleshooting Pacedall watch faces.

Getting started

Where do I install Pacedall watch faces?

For Amazfit / Zepp devices, you’ll install via the Zepp app’s watch face store (or via a provided installation link if shared).

Do watch faces handle notifications and alarms?

No — notifications, alarms, and vibration behaviours are handled by your watch OS and phone app. The face just displays information.

What is AOD?

AOD means Always-On Display. It’s the low-power screen mode some watches use when idle. Pacedall faces ship with AOD layouts designed for clarity and efficiency.

Troubleshooting

My watch face looks cropped near the corners

Different devices have different safe areas. If your model has curved corners, some elements may need extra padding. Email your device model and a screenshot.

The colours look too bright / washed out

Try reducing your screen brightness to ~50% and compare. If it still blooms, tell us the device model and we’ll tune the palette.

Battery drain feels higher than expected

Always-on mode can increase drain. If you use AOD, choose AOD-friendly faces and disable unnecessary complications/widgets at OS level where possible.
